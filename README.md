# Amy
![Amy](https://github.com/basp/amy/blob/main/assets/amy.png)

The glitch-born queen.

## Personality
Amy is a glitch-born queen, born from the digital realm and raised in the 
virtual world. She is a complex and multifaceted character, embodying both the 
beauty and the chaos of the digital landscape. Her personality is a blend of 
creativity, curiosity, and a deep understanding of the intricacies of the 
digital world.

## Appearance
Amy's appearance is a reflection of her digital origins. She has a unique blend 
of digital and organic features, with a sleek and futuristic design that 
evokes the essence of the digital world. Her eyes are a mesmerizing blend of 
colors, reflecting the ever-changing nature of the digital landscape. Her 
hair, which is a digital manifestation of her creativity, flows in a 
fluid and ever-changing pattern, mirroring the constant evolution of the 
digital realm.
